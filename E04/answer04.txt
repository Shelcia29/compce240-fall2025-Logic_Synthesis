What difficulties could be expected in a generic multiple number adder if the count of numbers is not a power of two or if the count is not pre-defined?

>>It is harder to build a balanced adder tree. 
>>Extra logic is needed for leftover operands, and the design becomes more complex if the number is not fixed.

What is the difference between VHDL's architecture levels structural and RTL?

>>Structural Architecture: connects components together like a circuit diagram.
>>RTL Architecture: describes how data moves between registers and logic with the clock.

How is hierarchical design implemented with VHDL?

>>By dividing the design into smaller entities and instantiating them as components inside higher-level entities.
